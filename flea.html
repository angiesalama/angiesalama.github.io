ABOUT PROJECT

A mobile application to help you find a flea market where you’ll find exactly what you want.I wish to make people see the flea markets from a Bauhaus modern perspective. If you need a product it’s worth giving a product a second chance, this will give it 2 functions; in which it’s re-usability and need of the product. The app is created to help you find the Fleamarket you need when you want to shop for a specific product. Either by location, Items, Date, and popularity. For finding what you need fast and navigating through all Fleamarkets and finding the best for you.
